Roast Chicken with
Herbs & Lemon

smittenkitchen.com — imported 2026-04-14

Prep
15m
Cook
1h 20m
Serves
4
US
Metric
Ingredients
  • Whole chicken1 (~4 lb) / 1.8 kg
  • Unsalted butter, softened3 tbsp / 42 g
  • Fresh thyme4 sprigs
  • Fresh rosemary2 sprigs
  • Lemon, halved1 large
  • Garlic cloves, smashed6 cloves
  • Olive oil2 tbsp / 30 ml
  • Kosher salt1½ tsp
Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Pat the chicken completely dry with paper towels — this is what gives you crispy skin.

  2. Mix softened butter with chopped thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Loosen skin over the breast and rub butter directly onto the meat.

  3. Stuff the cavity with lemon halves and garlic. Truss legs loosely and tuck wings under.

  4. Roast breast-side up for 60–80 minutes until thigh reads 165°F (74°C). Rest 15 minutes before carving.
