Roast Chicken
with Herbs & Lemon

Prep15 min
Cook1 hr 20 min
Serves4
US
Metric

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken1 (about 4 lb) / 1.8 kg
  • Unsalted butter, softened3 tbsp / 42 g
  • Fresh thyme4 sprigs
  • Fresh rosemary2 sprigs
  • Lemon, halved1 large
  • Garlic cloves, smashed6 cloves
  • Olive oil2 tbsp / 30 ml
  • Kosher salt1½ tsp / 7 ml
  • Black pepper½ tsp

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Pat the chicken completely dry with paper towels — this is what gives you crispy skin.

  2. Mix softened butter with chopped thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Carefully loosen the skin over the breast and rub butter directly onto the meat.

  3. Stuff the cavity with lemon halves and garlic. Truss the legs loosely with kitchen twine and tuck the wings under.

  4. Roast breast-side up for 60–80 minutes, until a thermometer in the thickest part of the thigh reads 165°F (74°C).

  5. Rest uncovered on a cutting board for 15 minutes before carving.
