Roast Chicken with Herbs & Lemon

smittenkitchen.com — imported 2026-04-14

Prep Time
15 min
Cook Time
1h 20m
Serves
4
Ingredients
  • Whole chicken1 (~4 lb)/ 1.8 kg
  • Unsalted butter3 tbsp/ 42 g
  • Fresh thyme4 sprigs
  • Fresh rosemary2 sprigs
  • Lemon, halved1 large
  • Garlic cloves6 cloves
  • Olive oil2 tbsp/ 30 ml
  • Kosher salt1½ tsp
Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Pat the chicken completely dry — this is what gives you crispy skin.

  2. Mix softened butter with chopped thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Rub butter under the skin directly onto the meat.

  3. Stuff the cavity with lemon and garlic. Truss legs and tuck wings under.

  4. Roast breast-side up 60–80 min until thigh reads 165°F (74°C). Rest 15 min before carving.
