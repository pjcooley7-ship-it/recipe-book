Roast Chicken with Herbs & Lemon

smittenkitchen.com — imported April 14, 2026

Prep
15 min
Cook
1h 20m
Serves
4

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken1 (~4 lb)/ 1.8 kg
  • Unsalted butter3 tbsp/ 42 g
  • Fresh thyme4 sprigs
  • Fresh rosemary2 sprigs
  • Lemon, halved1 large
  • Garlic cloves6 cloves
  • Olive oil2 tbsp/ 30 ml
  • Kosher salt1½ tsp

Instructions

  1. 1

    Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Pat the chicken completely dry with paper towels — this is what gives you crispy skin.

  2. 2

    Mix softened butter with chopped thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Loosen skin over the breast and rub butter directly onto the meat.

  3. 3

    Stuff the cavity with lemon halves and garlic. Truss legs loosely and tuck wings under.

  4. 4

    Roast breast-side up for 60–80 minutes until thigh reads 165°F (74°C). Rest 15 minutes before carving.
